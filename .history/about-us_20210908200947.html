About Us

Our Name

Cinctamore - a combined word of “cincta (surrounded, encircled, crowned, girded, enclosed)” and “amore (love)” means “surrounded by love.”


Our name reflects our belief that, the greatest of what is possible can only be achieved when what we do as a team is surrounded with love. In all we do, we strive to be a ‘Pattern of Good Work’. At Cinctamore, we are constantly finding solutions to support a better future for all.

History

Cinctamore was founded in 2018 to spearhead the development of multiple family-owned businesses by providing the framework to guide each business to stay competitive and successful.

Cinctamore aims to become a global business collaborator and solutions finder through the Architecture, Urban Design, Engineering & Construction, Trading & Investment, as well as Multimedia, Lifestyle and Health Groups.
